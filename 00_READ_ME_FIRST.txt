📞 TROUBLESHOOTING QUICK SOLUTIONS
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Issue: "Port 5000 already in use"
Solution: Kill process or use different port:
  netstat -ano | findstr :5000
  taskkill /PID <PID> /F

Issue: "Module not found" after restart
Solution: Activate virtual environment:
  ..\.venv\Scripts\Activate.ps1

Issue: "Can't access from phone"
Solution: Check firewall allows port 5000, use correct IP, ensure same Wi-Fi

Issue: "Voice not working"
Solution: Use Chrome/Edge, enable microphone, speak clearly

Issue: "PDF not generating"
Solution: Ensure invoices/ folder exists, check folder permissions
